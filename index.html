About Me

Passionate developer with a love for innovation and continuous learning

Building the Future, One Line at a Time

I'm a dedicated Full Stack Developer with a passion for technology and innovation. Fresh from graduating with an Ordinary Diploma in Information Technology Engineering from the Dar es Salaam Institute of Technology, I've built a solid foundation in both software and hardware technologies.

My journey spans across three dynamic organizations: Bizy Tech Limited where I completed my foundational internship, Amani Warehouse Limited where I currently drive web development initiatives, and MUA Insurance Tanzania where I'm expanding my expertise in computer engineering and networking.

I specialize in creating full-stack applications that solve real-world problems. From intuitive front-end interfaces to robust back-end systems, I believe in the power of clean code and user-centered design to create meaningful digital experiences.

When I'm not immersed in code, you'll find me exploring emerging technologies, contributing to the local developer community in Tanzania, and sharing knowledge with fellow tech enthusiasts. I'm driven by the endless possibilities that technology offers to make a positive impact.
